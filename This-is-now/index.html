Three consecutive evenings of inspired art, projected on well-known
landemarks in Boston, expressing the feelings and though of talented
artists for humainty teens responding via painting, photography, graphic design
and mixed media to out highly "intersectional" times. This is now!
offers afh teen artists an opportunity to reflect upon the issues of our times,
specifically in response to life during covid-19 and most importantly, to the
murder of george floyd and deaths of other black civilians at the hands of
police, systemic racism, and social justice movement. These issues
impact all young people and the future they are inheriting. This is now!
provides a platform for their voices to be heard.

Each evenings features a unique show, mapped to three boston neighborhood
buildings, and viewable live of via live stream on artists for humanityl's
facebook page.
